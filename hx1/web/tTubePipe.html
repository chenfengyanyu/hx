Titanium Tube&Pipe

3150t horizontal extrusion press,1000t vertical piercing machine and series of tube rolling mill make it possible to produce various of extruded and rolled seamless tubes of titanium.

Seamless tube and pipe. manufactured by cold rolling process from extruded pipe blanks and annealed in vacuum annealing furnace, have good quality and varied outside diameter and wall-thickness.

 

Grade: ASTM: Gr1,Gr2,Gr3 Gr7,Gr9, Gr12
Standard: ASTM B337,ASME37,ASTM B338,ASME338, ASTMB861
Size: Outside diameter:9mm-85mm
Wall thickness: 0.5mm-8.5mm
Length:1000mm-6000mm(20feet) to 12000mm(40feet)
Finish: Cold rolled and annealed in vacuum Anealing furnace
Applications: aviation, oceanic industries, petrochemical engineering, oil and gas, autocrat, power plant heat exchanger and so on.
Test: Flattening test, Tension test, Flaring test
