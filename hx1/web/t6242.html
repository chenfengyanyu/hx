Ti-6-2-4-2

 

Common Name: Ti-6-2-4-2
Ti-6Al-2Sn-4Zr-2Mo
UNS Number: R54620

General Information:

Ti-6Al-2Sn-4Zr-2Mo is used for forgings and rolled products in jet engines and airframe applications where high strength, toughness, and creep resistance are required. This near alpha alloy is available in bar, billet, castings, sheet, strip, and wire.
