AMC Titanium Metal Products

Advanced Metals Co., Ltd. is the manufacturer with nearly 20 years experiences for producing all kinds of titanium metal products. We produce all grades of C.P. Titanium and most of Titanium alloys according to ASTM, ASME, AMS, AWS,DIN, ISO specification. Our products system include of titanium and titanium alloy Ingot, Billet, Rolled Bar, Rod, Wires, Foils, Plates and Sheets, Seamless Tube and Pipe, Forged Ring and Disc, formed parts and Fasteners as customers request. We control all the producing step from the raw material to the finished products. We melt the titanium ingot by ourselves for use the pure titanium sponge. We never use the titanium scrap as the raw material to produce. All the producing work are in our own factory. We also have the equipment for other service, such as the cut, polish, package and so on. All of our titanium metals products are accept the third party test, such as SGS test. We also will sign the quality confirm contract to make sure all of our titanium metal product’s quality is good.

We honor every customer and order, we never allow our customers lose their values because our products’ quality or service.
