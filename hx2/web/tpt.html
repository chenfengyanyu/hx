Titanium Pipe & Tube

3150t horizontal extrusion press, 1000t vertical piercing machine and series of tube rolling mill make it possible to produce various of extruded and rolled seamless tubes of titanium seamless/welded tube and pipe. Manufactured by cold rolling process from extruded pipe blanks and annealed in vacuum annealing furnace, have good quality and varied outside diameter and wall-thickness.
